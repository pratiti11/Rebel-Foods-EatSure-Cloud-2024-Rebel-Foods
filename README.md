
# Team Stree – EatSure Campus Growth Strategy

A product strategy project focused on increasing the market penetration of EatSure in college campuses through research, user insights, and product design improvements.

This project analyzes the current challenges faced by EatSure and proposes solutions through marketing strategies, user experience redesign, and student-focused features.



---

# Overview

EatSure, a direct-to-consumer food delivery platform by Rebel Foods, already has a strong presence among young users. However, its penetration across serviceable college campuses is currently low.

This project proposes a comprehensive strategy to increase adoption among students by understanding their behavior, improving the app experience, and designing targeted marketing initiatives.

The research identifies key student needs such as affordable meals, group ordering convenience, and exclusive student discounts.

---

# Problem Statement

Although a large percentage of EatSure users are under 25, the app has only about **5% penetration in serviceable college campuses**. The challenge is to encourage students to choose EatSure over competitors like Swiggy and Zomato by improving awareness, usability, and incentives. 

---

# Research & Insights

Survey insights collected from students revealed several important behaviors:

* Many students were **not aware of the EatSure app**
* Students frequently **order food in groups**
* **Discounts and free delivery** strongly influence ordering decisions
* The most common spending range per order is **₹100 – ₹500**
* Peak ordering time is usually **dinner hours**

These insights guided the design of new product features and marketing strategies.

---

# Proposed Strategy

The project proposes a step-by-step strategy to increase adoption in college campuses.

## Market Research

* Analyze student ordering behavior
* Study competitor strategies
* Identify gaps in campus engagement

## Marketing Campaigns

* Social media engagement through memes and contests
* Collaboration with student influencers
* Campus ambassador programs

## Promotions & Offers

* Student-exclusive discounts
* Referral reward systems
* Bundled meal offers
* Limited time flash sales

## Campus Collaborations

* Pop-up stalls during college festivals
* Event sponsorships
* Partnerships with campus cafeterias

## Continuous Feedback

* In-app surveys
* Regular updates based on student feedback

---

# Product Features Proposed

### Group Ordering

Allow multiple users to add items to a shared cart and place one combined order.

### Split Payments

Enable friends to split payments directly inside the app.

### Student Specials

Students verify their ID to unlock exclusive discounts and offers.

### Personalized Recommendations

Use order history to recommend meals based on preferences.

### One-Click Ordering

Allow quick ordering for frequently purchased meals.

---

# UX Improvements

The project also proposes improvements to the EatSure interface:

* Minimal and cleaner interface
* Bottom navigation for easier navigation
* Personalized home screen
* Filter options for cuisine, price, and dietary preferences
* Gamification elements to encourage engagement

Good UI design can significantly improve usability and productivity for users. 

---

# User Personas

Two key student personas were identified:

### Busy Student

Needs quick ordering, affordable meals, and personalized recommendations.

### Group Orderer

Often orders with friends and needs features like shared carts, split payments, and group discounts.

These personas guided the development of the proposed features.

---

# Expected Impact

If implemented, the proposed strategies could:

* Increase EatSure awareness in college campuses
* Improve student engagement and retention
* Encourage higher group orders
* Increase average order value
* Strengthen EatSure’s position against competitors
